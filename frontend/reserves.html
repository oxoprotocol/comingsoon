How to Verify Independently
1
Check BTC Custody Balance
Visit mempool.space and look up the BTC multisig address. The confirmed balance is the total BTC backing oxoBTC.
https://mempool.space/testnet/address/{btcCustodyAddress}
2
Check oxoBTC Total Supply On-Chain
Call totalSupply() on the oxoBTC ERC-20 contract (8 decimals = satoshi precision).
Result ÷ 108 = total oxoBTC in circulation.
Read Contract on Etherscan
3
Calculate Collateral Ratio
Collateral Ratio = BTC balance (sats) / oxoBTC totalSupply (sats) × 100%
A healthy protocol maintains ≥ 100% at all times. Pending redemptions reduce the effective supply.
4
Verify Pending Redemptions
Query RedeemRequested events on the Bridge contract and call getRedeemInfo(redeemHash) for each. State 0 = Pending, 1 = Processing, 2 = Completed, 3 = Cancelled.
View Bridge Events on Etherscan
